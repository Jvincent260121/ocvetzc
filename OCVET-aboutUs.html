The Office of the City Veterinarian (OCVet) in Zamboanga City is committed to promoting animal health and public safety. Under the leadership of Dr. Mario D. Arriola, our team strives to deliver top-notch veterinary services to the community.

MISSION

To ensure the well-being of animals and safeguard public health through dedicated veterinary services and community education.

VISION

Community where all animals are healthy, cared for, and valued, contributing to the overall safety and harmony of Zamboanga City.
